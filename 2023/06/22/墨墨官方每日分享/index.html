​ 欲穷千里目，更上一层楼。

​ To thoroughly enjoy a thousand-mile sight, climb up another level.

​

​ 宝石不经磨难，不会有光泽；人不经历困苦，难以成才。

​ A gem cannot be polished without friction, nor a man perfected without trails.

​

​ 骐骥一跃，不能十步；驽马十驾，功在不舍。

​ The finest thoroughbred cannot travel ten paces in one leap, but the sorriest nag can go a ten day’s journey.

​

​ 读书如交友，应求少而精。

​ Books and friends should be few but good.

​

​ 宁可曾经爱过而失去，也不要从未爱过。

​ It’s better to have loved and lost than never to have loved at all.

​

​ 少年易老学难成，一寸光阴不可轻。

​ For youth, old age comes readily, yet knowledge is difficult to acquire.

​

​ 想要被爱，就要去爱别人，并让自己可爱。

​ If you would be loved, love and be lovable.

​

​ 天下兴亡，匹夫有责。

​ Survival of a Nation Is the Responsibility of Every Individual.

​

​ 勇敢离岸，方能到达新天地。

​ You cannot swim for new horizons until you have courage to lose sight of the shore.

​

​ 登山不是为了世界看到你，而是为了让你看到世界。

​ Climb the mountains not so the world can see you, but so you can see the world.

​

​ 修竹比顽橡更强大，因为它极其柔韧。

​ The bamboo that bends is stronger than the oak that resists.

​

​ 逆境中，有人筑墙，有人造风车。

​ When the wind of change blows, some people build walls, and others build windmils.

​

​ 面朝阳光，阴影就会落在身后。

​ Keep your face to the sunshine and you cannot see the shadow.

​

​ 每个艺术家都曾是业余爱好者。

​ Every artist was first an amateur.

​

​ 患难见真情。

​ A friend in need is a friend indeed.

​

​ 只有在字典里，成功才会出现在奋斗之前。

​ The only place where success comes before work is in the dictionary.

​

​ 欲速则不达。

​ Haste makes waste.

​

​ 爱不是用眼睛去看，而是用心灵感受。

​ Love looks not with the eyes, but with the mind.

​

​ 君子慎独，不欺暗室。

​ Integrity is doing the right thng, even when no one is watching.

​

​ 苍天不负有心人。

​ All good things come to those who wait.

​

​ 真理就像太阳。你可以暂时遮住它，但它不会消失。

​ Truth is like the sun. You can shut it out for a time, but it won’t go away.

​

​ 广交友，无深交。

​ A friend to all is a friend to none.

​

​ 善良无言也无形，却可以被所有人感知到。

​ Kindness is the language which the deaf can hear and the blind can see.

​

​ 物以类聚，人以群分。

​ Birds of a feather flock together.

​

​ 你人生中最重要的两天是你出生的那天和你找到人生意义的那天。

​ The two most important days in your life are the day you are born and the day you find out why.

​

​ 我们能够成长的唯一途径

​ The only way that we can grow is if we change.

​

​ 毅力不是一场长跑，而是一场又一场的短跑。

​ Perseverance is not a long race; it is many short races one after the other.

​

​ 不要过早打如意算盘。

​ Don’t count your chickens before they hatch.

​

​ 说起来容易做起来难。

​ Easier said than done.

​

​ 力量不来自身体机能，而来自不屈不挠的意志。

​ Strength does not come from physical capacity. It comes from an indomitable will.

​

​ 否极泰来。

​ Every cloud has a silver lining.

​

​ 只有敢于冒险走的更远的人，才能知道自己能走多远。

​ Only those who will risk going too far can possibly find out how far one can go.

​

​ 真诚是智慧之书的第一章。

​ Honesty is the first chapter in the book of wisdom.

​

​ 坚持不懈能够把失败变成超凡成就。

​ Persistence can change failure into extraordinary achievement.

​

​ 生活就像骑自行车，保持平衡得靠行进。

​ Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance, you must keep moving.

​

​ 没有什么可以代替奋斗。

​ There is no substitute for hard work.

​

​ 读书之于思维就像运动之于身体一样重要。

​ Reading is to the mind what exercise is to the body.

​

​ 英雄是为了比自己更大的信仰而奉献了自己生命的人。

​ A hero is someone who has given his or her life to something bigger than oneself.

​

​ 我们学得越多，就越能发现自己的无知。

​ The more we study, the more we discover our ignorance.

​

​ 学习永远不会让思想疲惫。

​ Learning never exhausts the mind.

​

​ 爱自己是终身浪漫的开始。

​ To love oneself is the beginning of a lifelong romance.

​

​ 未来属于那些今天为它做好准备的人。

​ The future belongs to those who prepare for it today.

​

​ 年龄不是障碍，它只是你在思想上加诸于自己的限制。

​ Age is not a barrier. It’s a limitation you put on your mind.

​

​ 爱不是占有，而是欣赏。

​ Love is not about possession. It’s about appreciation.

​

​ 不要让对衰老的恐惧阻止你尝试新事物，尽情享受生活吧。

​ Don’t let the fear of aging hold you back from trying new things and living life to the fullest.

​

​ 不冒风险取胜就无法光荣地成功。

​ To win without risk is to triumph without glory.

​

​ 光有才华是不够的，你需要知道如何运用它。

​ It’s not enough to have talent. You need to know how to use it.
